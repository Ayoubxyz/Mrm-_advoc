Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our legal services and consultation process

Do you offer free initial consultations?

Yes, we offer a complimentary 30-minute initial consultation for most legal matters. This allows us to understand your situation and provide preliminary advice on the best course of action. Complex commercial matters may require a more detailed consultation.

How are your legal fees structured?

Our fee structure varies depending on the type of legal service required. We offer hourly rates, fixed fees for routine matters, and contingency fees where appropriate. All fees are discussed and agreed upon before we begin work on your matter, ensuring complete transparency.

What should I bring to my first consultation?

Please bring any relevant documents related to your legal matter, including contracts, correspondence, court papers, or other materials. A chronological summary of events can also be helpful. If you're unsure what to bring, our team can guide you when scheduling your appointment.

Do you handle cases outside of Nairobi?

Yes, we represent clients throughout Kenya and handle matters in courts across the country. For clients outside Nairobi, we can arrange video consultations and coordinate with local resources as needed. We also have experience with cross-border matters involving international law.

How long does it typically take to resolve a legal matter?

The timeline for resolving legal matters varies significantly depending on the complexity of the case, court schedules, and whether settlement is possible. During your consultation, we'll provide a realistic timeline estimate based on the specifics of your situation.

Can you help with urgent legal matters?

Yes, we understand that legal emergencies can arise at any time. We provide 24/7 emergency legal services for urgent matters such as arrests, injunctions, or time-sensitive business issues. Contact our emergency line for immediate assistance.
