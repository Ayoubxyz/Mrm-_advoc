Our Story

Founded by three visionary legal minds - Mohamed, Rama, and Mursal - MRM Advocates LLP has grown from a boutique practice to one of Kenya's most respected law firms. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide exceptional legal services that combine deep local knowledge with international best practices.

For over two decades, we have been at the forefront of Kenya's legal landscape, helping individuals, businesses, and institutions navigate complex legal challenges. Our commitment to excellence, integrity, and innovation has earned us the trust of clients across diverse industries and sectors.

Today, MRM Advocates LLP stands as a testament to the power of collaborative excellence, where experienced attorneys work together to deliver results that exceed client expectations. We pride ourselves on our ability to provide strategic legal solutions that drive business success and protect individual rights.
